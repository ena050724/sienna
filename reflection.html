Return to The Ocean

Reflection

I have always found fishing very interesting, and one of my unrealized dreams is to catch legendary fish that do not exist in real life. Because of this, I created a small interactive world where users can experience catching mythical sea creatures.

I intentionally designed the fish animations to feel slightly stretched and unusual, to suggest that these are not ordinary fish but special or supernatural beings.

I changed the cursor into a fishing rod to make the interaction feel more immersive, and I added moving bubbles and water motion to create the feeling of being underwater. I also expanded the screen space to give the impression of swimming inside a wide ocean environment rather than looking at a small scene.

At the beginning, I struggled a lot with keyframes and fish movement. The fish sometimes moved strangely, teleported, or swam diagonally too fast like rockets. However, I later realized that some of these unexpected movements actually made the creatures feel more unique and interesting than normal swimming behaviour, so I kept parts of that effect intentionally.

In future development, I would like to improve the bubble movement further and add a stronger pulling animation when the fishing rod catches a fish. Overall, I found designing this website a very enjoyable experience, and it made me interested in learning more about interactive web design in the future.
