Interface Gestures

This page documents everyday interface gestures I performed across different environments and devices over a 12-hour period. The gestures are organised by context to show how interaction behaviour shifts between phone, transport systems, buildings, laptop, and home space!

Phone

  • tap alarm off
  • scroll TikTok
  • swipe notifications
  • tap camera
  • type messages

Transport

  • tap Myki

Building access

  • swipe door card
  • press elevator button

Laptop

  • scroll Canvas
  • drag files

Home

  • switch motion-light by jumping
  • plug charger
